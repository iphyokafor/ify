# Demo Flow

1. **Sign up or log in**
   - Use demo accounts: `bob`, `alice`, `charlie`, `diana`, `eve` (password: `password123`).

2. **Home feed**
   - See your tweets and tweets/reposts from people you follow.
   - Post a tweet (composer at the top).

3. **Like and repost**
   - Like or repost any tweet. UI updates instantly.

4. **Profiles**
   - Click a username or go to `/u/<username>` to view a profile.
   - Follow/unfollow users. Stats update right away.

5. **Search**
   - Use the search bar to find users and follow/unfollow from results.

6. **Delete**
   - Delete your own tweet from the feed or your profile.
